Tennis Sports

Sport Overview
Tennis, also known as lawn tennis, is a popular team sport played on a field. It involves two players trying to score points by playing a ball into the opponent's court .

Rules and Regulations
Tennis has specific rules governing gameplay, such as deuce, fouls, and advantage. Understanding these rules is essential for fair and competitive play.

Equipment and Gear
Tennis players typically wear jerseys during matches and use rackets. The only required equipment is the rackets and a ball, making it accessible to players of all ages.

Playing Field
Tennis is played on a field with opponents at each end. The field is marked with lines for various purposes which includes serving line and net.
